Varax is now running!

Your cluster is being scanned for SOC2 compliance.

Quick start:

1. Check compliance score:
   kubectl exec -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} deploy/{{ include "varax.fullname" . }} -- varax scan --output styled

2. View metrics:
   kubectl port-forward -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} svc/{{ include "varax.fullname" . }} 8080:8080
   curl http://localhost:8080/metrics | grep varax

3. Check operator status:
   kubectl get complianceconfigs -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}
